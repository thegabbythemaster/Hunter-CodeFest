In this educational game platform, we recommend you click on learning the terms first.

Starting the game will teach you the 6 climate types and 7 energy types. Or you can skip this part.

When you click on play scenarios after starting the game and clicking skip, you will be directed to a page to pick different locations.

The goal is to pick environmentally friendly choices to win the game. Continuously choosing bad options will lead you to lose.

Good Luck!
